Development of this site

March 2, 2022

One Hundred Years of Solitude is a vast book, every page of this book contains vital details which the reader cannot afford to miss. I finished reading this book in January 2022 and then decided to make a wiki for it.

As you know from previous blog posts that I am currently studying so I am not working full time on this site. But I will try to add as many details as I could. To make a wiki this novel would require re-reading time and again. Though I enjoyed this novel and I hope I would read between the lines to get more information out of it.

Since this book belongs to the Magical Realism genre, it contains both magic and reality in itself. Throughout this novel we get to see unusually fantastic things happening and also some tangibaly real incidents like massacre of the worker of the Banana Company which resembles to Banana Massacre happened in Colombia, 1928.

This site may take a long time to be complete or I imagine if it ever be completed because new interpretations will be made and new information will be mined from the novel. But I've decided to keep this side project alive as long as I could as a service to literature and Art.

Thank you for reading.
